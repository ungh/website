Recruitment Process

The selection process includes a stage of pre-screening together with a phone interview.
The second step provides a motivational-attitudinal interview during which the personal characteristics, the professional experiences gained and the language skills will be deepened.
Finally a techical interview with the Head of Department for evaluating the technical and specific skilld in line to the vacant position.
