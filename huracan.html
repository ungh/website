The Lamborghini Huraan is a sports car built by Lamborghini, replacing Lamborghini's sales leader and most produced car, the Gallardo. The Huraan made its auto show debut at the March 2014 Geneva Auto Show, and was released in the second quarter of 2014.

ENGINE

Type: V10
Displacement: 5,204 cm
Bore and stroke: 84,5 mm x 92,8 mm
Valve gear: Variable valve timing electronically controlled
Compression ratio: m12.7 : 1
Maximum power: 610 CV (449 kW) @ 8,250 RPM
Maximum torque: 560 Nm (412 lbft) @ 6,500 RPM
Emission class: EURO 6 - LEV 2
Emission control system: Catalytic converters with lambda sensors
Cooling system: Water and oil cross flow cooling system with fixed air intakes
Engine management system: Bosch MED 17 Master Slave
Lubrification system: Dry sump
